About Me

Hi! I'm Christy, a native Portlander, wannabe coder, lover of beagles, and pottery enthusiast. I'm also an ex-JW (Jehovah Witness) and this blog is dedicated to telling my story about leaving and moving on.

Welcome to my blog!

Here's my story in thirty seconds or less:

I was raised by Jehovah Witness parents, and was baptized at 16. I was very good witness girl and daughter. Despite "losing" fleshly sisters and friends to "the world," throughout my teen years and early 20s, I was very active in meetings and field service. I was definitely drinking the kool-aid and firmly believed every Watchtower teaching. In my mid 20s some things about the organization started to bother me. But instead of leaving, I decided to become a full time minister, hoping it would help me make up my mind. Shortly after, I resigned from the organization and started a new life free of control and inauthenticity.
